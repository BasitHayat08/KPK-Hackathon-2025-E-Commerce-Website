About ShopStake

ShopStake is a hackathon demo e-commerce website built with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Vanilla JavaScript. Our goal is to provide a **fast, responsive, and aesthetic shopping experience**.

We include all professional features such as:

  • Product listing with search, category, and price filters
  • Cart management with live badge and totals
  • Checkout form with order summary
  • Smooth animations and responsive design

Built for speed, elegance, and hackathon glory!
